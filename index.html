Privacy Policy – AgriCare

Effective Date: September 4, 2025

Welcome to AgriCare, a mobile application that provides tools for aspiring farmers such as crops and livestock information, weather insights, calculators, and farm recording.

Quick Summary

  • We do not require you to create an account and we do not sell your data.
  • Core features work offline. Some features, such as weather updates require internet connectivity.
  • We may request approximate location to provide local weather, if you allow it. You can deny this and enter a location manually (if supported by your build).

1. Information We Collect

  • Approximate Location (optional): If you grant permission, we may access your device’s location to tailor weather forecasts to your area. Location is used only for providing content like local weather and is not stored long-term by the App.
  • Diagnostic Data (optional in your store build): Your app store or OS may provide anonymized crash reports or performance metrics. We use these to improve stability.
  • In-App Content: Notes, farm records, or calculator inputs you add are stored locally on your device unless you export/share them. We do not upload your content to our servers.

No Sensitive Categories: We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal data (e.g., health, financial, biometric) beyond what you voluntarily input into the App’s tools.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Local-First: Most data (e.g., your notes, preferences) is stored on your device.
  • No Sale of Data: We do not sell or rent your personal information.
  • Your Controls: You can revoke permissions (Location, Notifications) in your device settings at any time.

4. App Permissions

  • Location: to provide local weather (approximate or precise, depending on your choice).
  • Notifications: to receive alerts (e.g., weather advisories, reminders).
  • Network/Internet: to fetch weather data or updates from allowed services.
  • Storage: to cache content and store your offline data.

5. Third-Party Services

  • WeatherAPI (weatherapi.com): used to fetch weather data for your selected location(s). Requests may include coarse location or place names. We do not send personal identifiers.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page, and the Effective Date will be revised accordingly.
